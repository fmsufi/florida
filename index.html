As the Foundation that owns Villa Flor de Cabrera completes its mission in Cabrera, Dominican Republic, and prepares to move on to new ventures, it hopes to find a visionary owner — a steward who understands that this is not just a breathtaking beachfront estate, but a powerful engine for philanthropic good.

A Proven Model

In 2008, the Foundation constructed Villa Flor de Cabrera and launched the Trade for Travel program. This initiative was designed to create meaningful partnerships with guests seeking a more impactful vacation experience. Through the program, guests who chose to donate to community projects, actively participate in local initiatives, or bring essential supplies were rewarded with rental credits. Since its inception, Trade for Travel has generated over $1 million to support educational and philanthropic efforts in Cabrera.

Cabrera Center
for Progress

The Cabrera Community Center stands as one of the Foundation’s most ambitious undertakings.

For nearly twenty years, the Foundation laid the groundwork for this center by supporting critical community pillars. The ultimate goal was to consolidate all these charitable efforts into a single, centralized hub.

Decades of groundwork included:

  • Teacher Training
  • K-12 School Establishment
  • Vocational Programs
  • Local Resident & Animal Care
In 2025, this vision became reality. The Cabrera Center for Progress is now complete, standing as a permanent hub for education and vocational training for the entire region.
